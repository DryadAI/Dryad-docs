## Overview

Dryad-docs is the canonical repository for all architectural documentation, identity baselines, and governance rules for the Dryad system. It serves as the "superego" and "memory" for all Dryad sub-projects and agents.

## Core Documents

- **[PILLARS.md](core/PILLARS.md):** The foundational matrix of Pillars and Schools.
- **[SOUL.md](core/SOUL.md):** The identity baseline and core design rules (R1–R16).
- **[AGENTS.md](core/AGENTS.md):** Instructions and constraints for AI agents.
- **[SUBPROJECT_PATTERN.md](core/SUBPROJECT_PATTERN.md):** The standard structure for all Dryad sub-projects.

## Schools of Thought

The Dryad system is organized into three Schools, each governing a specific aspect of system identity:

1. **[School of Memory](schools/memory/README.md):** Persistence, identity mirroring, and fact promotion.
2. **[School of Governance](schools/governance/README.md):** Evaluation, drift detection, and principled oversight.
3. **[School of Execution](schools/execution/README.md):** Action, tool-use, tracing, and reflection.
